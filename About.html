    HISTORY

    Google Developer Student Clubs were launched by Google in 2017.

    The aim was to create communities where university students from various backgrounds could come together to learn, share ideas, and collaborate on projects related to Google technologies.

    NATURE

    Over time, GDSC has evolved to adapt to the changing needs and trends in technology.

    New topics and technologies are incorporated into club activities to ensure that students are exposed to the latest advancements in the field.

    OUR MISSION & VISION

    To provide a platform for university students using Google technologies.

    GDSC aims to empower students with the skills and knowledge necessary to thrive.

    To inspire the next generation of developers and innovators.
