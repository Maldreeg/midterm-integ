    Empowering Communities through Technologies.

    GDSC aims to empower students with the skills and knowledge necessary to thrive in the rapidly evolving field of technology.

    WHAT'S GDSC?

    Google Developer Student Club Leads are passionate leaders at their universities who are
    dedicated to helping their peers learn and connect.

    Leads are undergraduate or graduate
    university students pursuing various degrees, yet have foundational knowledge of software
    development concepts.
